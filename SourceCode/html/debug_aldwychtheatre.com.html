Find us

On the corner of Drury Lane and the Aldwych, just off the Strand, the theatre is ideally located with transport links, hotels and restaurants close by.

We recommend getting to the theatre by public transport, if possible.

Main Foyer Staircase

The original finials which topped the bannisters of the main foyer staircase were lost to renovations over time, so in 2017 we recreated them using old photographs of the originals as reference.

Auditorium

Set across three levels, our Auditorium sits 1,122 people, with no supporting pillars to block anyone's view.
