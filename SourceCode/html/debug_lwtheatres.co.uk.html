About the Adelphi Theatre

The Adelphi Theatre is co-owned with the Nederlander Group. Throughout its fascinating 200-year history, the theatre’s gorgeous art deco interiors have hosted Andrew Lloyd Webber’s Sunset Boulevard, Phantom-sequel Love Never Dies, the record-breaking One Man Two Guvnors, gloriously glam-drag Kinky Boots, and Sara Bareilles‘ hit Broadway musical Waitress. Currently, the Adelphi is home to the stage adaptation of the classic Hollywood film Back To The Future – winner of the 2022 Olivier Award for Best New Musical.

Looking to hire the Adelphi Theatre?

With a plush auditorium that seats over 1400, the distinctive art deco surroundings of the Adelphi Theatre can accommodate any large-scale conference or product launch. Located slap-bang in the centre of The Strand, you’ll find the theatre just up the road from Trafalgar Square where our West End expertise, super-smooth hospitality and legendary stage will be on hand to ensure your event runs like a proper production.
